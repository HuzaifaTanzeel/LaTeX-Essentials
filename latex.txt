\section{INTRODUCTION}
The objective of this manual is to provide guidance for the setup and utilization of the Dawaai App on a daily basis.\hfill \break
The first section of this manual is dedicated to outlining the steps required to configure and install the Dawaai App on a mobile device.\hfill \break 
The second section of the manual provides an overview of the services offered by the app and elucidates the procedures required for their utilization.
The manual aims to serve as a comprehensive guide 
for users of the Dawaai App to enable them to make the most of its features and services.

\section{SERVICES}
Dawaai is an online healthcare management app in Pakistan that allows users to order medicines, book lab tests, consult with doctors, and access information about medicines and diseases. The app also offers home services such as nursing, dental, and physiotherapy. Users can consult with doctors through video appointments or chat with general physicians for free. Lab tests can be booked online, and the diagnostics team will collect samples from the user's home. \hfill \break
